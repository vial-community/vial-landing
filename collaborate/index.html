Become a Settler Program

Are you a crypto enthusiast who wants to take part in building the first Albanian DAO?

If yes, we have made the "Settler" program exactly for you. It does not matter where you are in the world as long as you have a passion for contributing to and growing crypto adaption.

First, we need you to read our Whitepaper 1.0 to understand our vision and what a "Settler" is. If you think you are a good fit then you can apply in the attached form.

What we expect from Settlers:

  • Help in growing our community across Telegram & Discord by inviting more like-minded people and sharing our project in other communities.
  • Contribute to publishing Albanian content about crypto & related fields in vial.community
  • Enforce ViAl values and crypto ethos in our community channels
  • Support ViAl social channels
  • Participate in our private & public governance discourse

What can you expect from us:

  • Access to all beta releases of ViAl Apps
  • Closer communication with the core team including meetups
  • Priority in ViAl events and discounts on exclusive merch
  • Dedicated "Settler" allocation of tokens

Become a Business Partner

VIAL is more than just a walking app. We are building the first crypto ecosystem serving Albanians wherever they are in the world, and VIAL App is the first step towards this goal.

VIAL will help onboard Albanian businesses into Web3, giving them the right tools to take part in the next generation of the internet and connect with global markets.

The perks of being a partner of VIAL at this stage are remarkable, but more on those will be delivered in the coming weeks to the interested parties filling out this form.
