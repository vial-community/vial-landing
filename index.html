Our Vision and Mission

VIAL walking is a WEB3 lifestyle APP that rewards your daily steps with a new-generation currency you can spend on cool products starting from buying vouchers of big brands to festival tickets and staking NFTs, all inside the VIAL marketplace.

Why? Because when you look after your health, you benefit society. You are more productive. After a day’s walk everything has twice its usual val ue.

Our core mission is to encourage you to get up and move outdoors, whether that’s moving towards a healthier you or moving to feel good.

How it works?

Turn your movement into mining sessions. The more you walk, run, jump, sweat and burn calories, the healthier you get, the better the rewards!

  1. Download and Sign-Up in VIAL APP
  2. Move outdoor and burn energy
  3. Earn handsome rewards

Welcome to VIAL! The revolutionized Virtual Albania.

You are joining the first Decentralized Autonomous Society (DAS) in the world, called Virtual Albania, or VIAL that will exist in the Metaverse but also connect the Albanian diaspora in real life. VIAL is established with the goal of progressively becoming a much more effective environment for economic, social, and cultural life in the Metaverse when compared to using traditional public and private institutions in the physical world. We are building a civilization based on kindness, fun, and economic freedom. VIAL's economy is driven by its own crypto token – VIALt, which will be issued progressively over the course of this transition in the next 100 years. Settlers – the members of the Society – breathe life into VIAL. We – the founders of the VIAL project – are the First Settlers – like the people who migrated from Europe to North America 500 years ago, we are migrating from Albania as a state to Virtual Albania in the Metaverse.
